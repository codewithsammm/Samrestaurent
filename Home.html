About My Restaurent & Hotel

Welcome to Sam Restaurant And Hotel, your perfect destination for delicious food and warm hospitality. Located in the heart of the city, we offer a wide variety of Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and love. Our goal is to give you a memorable dining experience with mouth-watering flavors and a cozy ambiance that feels just like home. Whether you're visiting with friends, family, or for a special occasion, Sam Restaurant is the ideal place to relax and enjoy tasty food at affordable prices. We believe in quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction. Come and explore our chef’s special recipes, quick service, and a comfortable atmosphere. Thank you for choosing us — we look forward to serving you!
